## Conditioning
They are doing this to me in many aspects, and that’s one of the reasons I say that they are not treating me as a human being.

The most insidious form of this twisted conditioning is probably the punishment for good behaviour. I have mentioned many times that I meant to give my best to the host countries, but got tormented for years in return. It’s not just that I got treated like garbage in general, I often face worse humiliation, insults and even health damage after doing something that, by my understanding of human decency, should be counted among good deeds.

Why would authorities do this to a human being when basically imprisoning him indefinitely? What’s the goal?

I wonder if real human readers can imagine what it feels like.
